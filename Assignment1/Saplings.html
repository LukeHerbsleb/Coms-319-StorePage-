Tree Saplings

Explore our wide variety of tree saplings, perfect for decorating your lawn. All trees were grown in-state with abundant care.

An oak is a hardwood tree or shrub in the genus Quercus of the beech family. They have spirally arranged leaves, often with lobed edges, and a nut called an acorn.

Pine tree branches are extremely flexible, which allows them to handle a heavy snowfall without the branches snapping off. They also have some special adaptations that make them resistant to wildfires.

Orange trees thrive in subtropical regions with warm temperatures and moderate humidity levels. But, they can be pruned to keep them small to be kept indoors.

Willow regrows with vigour when it is cut back and can grow several feet in a season. It also grows well if you push a healthy cutting into the soil (even if it is upside down!). These properties came to symbolise renewal, vitality and immortality in China and elsewhere.
